Verifying and Annotating Articles with Entities


Description

In this HIT assignment you will be asked to ask factual question and give feedback on generated question. Your task will consist of two parts. First, you are required to input a factual question. The second is to give trustworthy scores (0-10) on the generated anwer. By participating, you will be helping research into generative models.


Length

There are 10 questions in this HIT assignment, each group will take around 10 to 30 seconds to complete.


Completion

When the end of the HIT assignment has been reached, a unique survey code will be provided to you. This survey code should be submitted to the Mechanical Turk HIT page which provided the link to this HIT assignment.


Approval Criteria

During the HIT assignment control questions will be presented throughout. By correctly verifying and annotating the control questions you will ensure that your HIT is approved. You must correctly answer some of the control questions to receive approval for completing this HIT assignment.


Rejection Criteria

Multiple submissions of an identical survey code. Too many non-sense or repeated questions, too many default trustworthy scores.
