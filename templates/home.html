Verifying and Annotating Articles with Entities


Description

In this HIT assignment you will be shown short pieces of text drawn from news articles and other online sources. Some of the words will be highlighted already indicating the highlighted text maps to a particular Wikipedia article. Your task will consist of two parts. First, to verify that the Wikipedia article mapped to is correct. The second is to highlight new portions of text that you believe should map to a Wikipedia Article. By participating, you will be helping research into understanding online discourse.


Length

There are 10 questions in this HIT assignment, each group will take around 1 to 3 minutes to complete.


Completion

When the end of the HIT assignment has been reached, a unique survey code will be provided to you. This survey code should be submitted to the Mechanical Turk HIT page which provided the link to this HIT assignment.


Approval Criteria

During the HIT assignment control questions will be presented throughout. By correctly verifying and annotating the control questions you will ensure that your HIT is approved. You must correctly answer some of the control questions to receive approval for completing this HIT assignment.


Rejection Criteria

Multiple submissions of an identical survey code. Too many incorrect annotations on the control questions.
