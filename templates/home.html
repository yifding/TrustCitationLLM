How much do you trust ChatGPT?


Description

In this task, your job is to ask any question to ChatGPT and then judge how much you trust the response. This task consists of two parts: (1) You enter a question. (2) You provide a trust-score (0-10) about the response. By participating, you will be helping research into AI systems.


Length

There are 10 questions, each question will take around 20 to 30 seconds to complete.


Completion

At the end of this task, a unique code will be provided. This survey code should be submitted to final page which provided the link to this task.


Approval Criteria

You may complete this task multiple times. However, repeated, inappropriate, or apathetic questions will result in a task failure. Have fun with it.
