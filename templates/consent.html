Informed Consent

About This Research

You are being asked to participate in a research study. Scientists do research to answer questions and discover new information. Some research might help change or improve the way we do things in the future. This consent information will tell you more about the study to help you decide whether you want to participate. Please read this information before agreeing to be in the study.


Taking Part In This Study Is Voluntary

You may choose not to take part in the study or may choose to leave the study at any time. Deciding not to participate, or deciding to leave the study will not affect your relationship with the University of Notre Dame. As an alternative to participating in the study, you may choose not to take part. The study is being conducted by Tim Weninger at the University of Notre Dame (email: tweninger@nd.edu phone: 574-631-6770) This study is being funded by the DARPA under contracts HR001121C0168 and HR00112290106.


Why Is This Study Being Done?

This study asks how users trust the output of AI systems, like ChatGPT.


What Will Happen During The Study?

If you agree to be in the study, you will be asked to do the following things: You will input a question, that question will be sent to ChatGPT and then you will be asked to rate how much you trust the response. The response may take a moment (sometimes 10-15 seconds depending on the question).


What Are The Risks Of Taking Part In The Study?

Although unlikely, some texts may contain inappropriate and offensive language. We will simply pass back the response from ChatGPT.


How Long Will This Study Take?

This study will take about 10 - 15 minutes to complete.


How Will My Information Be Protected?

All questions will be sent to and likely stored by ChatGPT. Likewise responses and your ratings will be stored at Notre Dame. We will not collect any Personal Identifying Information (PII). Organizations that may inspect and/or copy your research records for quality assurance and data analysis include groups such as the study investigator and his/her research associates, the University of Notre Dame Institutional Review Board or its designees, DOD for quality assurance and data analysis, and (as allowed by law) state or federal agencies, especially the Office for Human Research Protections (OHRP), who may need to access the research records.


Will I be Paid?

You will be paid 3.00 USD for completion of this task. MTurk does not allow for prorated compensation. In the event of an incomplete HIT, you will not receive any compensation. This study contains a number of checks to make sure that participants are finishing the tasks honestly and completely. As long as you read the instructions and complete the tasks, your HIT will be approved. If you fail these checks, your HIT will be rejected and you will not receive any compensation.


Who Should I Contact With Questions Or Problems?

For questions about the study, contact the researcher Tim Weninger (email: tweninger@nd.edu) For questions about your rights as a research participant, to discuss problems, complaints, or concerns about a research study, please contact Notre Dame Research Compliance at 574-631-1461 or at compliance@nd.edu.


Participant's Consent
