About This Research

You are being asked to participate in a research study. Scientists do research to answer questions and discover new information. Some research might help change or improve the way we do things in the future. This consent information will tell you more about the study to help you decide whether you want to participate. Please read this information before agreeing to be in the study.


Taking Part In This Study Is Voluntary

You may choose not to take part in the study or may choose to leave the study at any time. Deciding not to participate, or deciding to leave the study later, will not result in any penalty and will not affect your relationship with the University of Notre Dame. As an alternative to participating in the study, you may choose not to take part. The study is being conducted by Tim Weninger at the University of Notre Dame (email: tweninge@nd.edu phone: 574-631-6770) This study is being funded by the DOD under proposal w911NF-17-1-0448.


Why Is This Study Being Done?

This study aims to verify how different datasets are used for evaluation. Current evaluation of machine learning models utilize ground truth datasets. We aim to see how people interpret the results of various models and ground truth datasets.


What Will Happen During The Study?

If you agree to be in the study, you will be asked to do the following things: We will be displaying a a short article taken from the news or other online sources. You will verify that the highlighted text maps to the appropriate Wikipedia article. Then, you will see if any other portions of the text should be highlighted and mapped to a Wikipedia article.


What Are The Risks Of Taking Part In The Study?

While participating in the study, the potential risks include: Some texts to be annotated may contain inappropriate and offensive language.


How Long Will This Study Take?

This study will take about 20 - 30 minutes to complete.


How Will My Information Be Protected?

Efforts will be made to keep your personal information confidential. We cannot guarantee absolute confidentiality. Your personal information may be disclosed if required by law. No information which could identify you will be shared in publications about this study and databases in which results may be stored. Organizations that may inspect and/or copy your research records for quality assurance and data analysis include groups such as the study investigator and his/her research associates, the University of Notre Dame Institutional Review Board or its designees, DOD for quality assurance and data analysis, and (as allowed by law) state or federal agencies, especially the Office for Human Research Protections (OHRP), who may need to access the research records.

Will You Share My Information With Other People?

All information collected from this study is stored in a secure password-protected database. Will I be Paid?

You will be paid $4.00 for completion of this task. MTurk does not allow for prorated compensation. In the event of an incomplete HIT, you will not receive any compensation. This study contains a number of checks to make sure that participants are finishing the tasks honestly and completely. As long as you read the instructions and complete the tasks, your HIT will be approved. If you fail these checks, your HIT will be rejected and you will not receive any compensation.
